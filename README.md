## Notes

### Blue Bluff Campground (Aberdeen, MS)

* Located on the Tennessee-Tombigbee Waterway near Aberdeen Lake.
* Scenic campground known for its limestone and clay bluffs overlooking the water.
* Features electric and water hookups, fishing access, swimming areas, and beautiful waterfront views.
